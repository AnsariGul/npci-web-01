What is UPI?

UPI (Unified Payments Interface) is a real-time payment system that helps in transferring funds between two bank accounts through a mobile platform. Through UPI, one can link multiple bank accounts with a single mobile application, without any hassle. It is the easiest way to transfer money instantly and safely, without having to share one’s bank details.
