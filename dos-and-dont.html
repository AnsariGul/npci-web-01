Dos and Don’ts while performing UPI and other digital payments/transactions

Do

  1. Be sure and attentive before you enter UPI PIN
  2. Report to bank if you spot any suspicious accounts
  3. Always buy products from reputed online merchants
  4. Always check for confirmation emails/SMS on completing transactions
  5. Keep track of all your SMSes/emails from the bank on your transactions
  6. Closely follow safety instructions and important announcements that banks and UPI enabled apps come up with

Don’t

  1. Share your card number
  2. Share your expiry date
  3. Share your CVV
  4. Share your UPI PIN
  5. Share your OTP you receive while performing digital transactions
